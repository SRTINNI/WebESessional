Game Rules

  • Rock crushes Scissors
  • Scissors cuts Paper

Payoff Matrix Explanation:

  • Winning: 1 (Player gains 1 point)
  • Loss: -1 (Player loses 1 point)
  • Tie: 0 (No change in score)
